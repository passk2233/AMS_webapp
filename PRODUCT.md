# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students use the app on mobile to evaluate teachers for the subjects in their current class group. Admin users review completion and score summaries by teacher, subject, and class, then open printable reports when needed.

## Product Purpose

This app collects teacher evaluation scores, comments, and completion status for AMS_UP. Success means students can submit every required question quickly and admins can see who taught what, which class evaluated it, and how many students have responded.

## Brand Personality

Clear, academic, reliable. The interface should feel calm and official, with familiar controls and enough density for repeated administrative work.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style dashboards, decorative card grids, oversized hero layouts, unclear custom controls, and visual treatments that make the form feel playful instead of institutional.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next task obvious for students and admins.
2. Keep form controls familiar, large enough for mobile, and easy to verify before submitting.
3. Show administrative hierarchy in the same order the school thinks about it: teacher, subject, class, completion.
4. Preserve Lao language readability and avoid low-contrast muted text.
5. Favor compact, scannable data over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Lao typography, high contrast body text, visible focus states, touch targets of at least 44px, and layouts that work on narrow mobile screens. Motion should be minimal and respect reduced-motion preferences.
